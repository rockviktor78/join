Welcome to the Help section of Join. Here you will find everything you need to get started with our kanban-based project management tool.

What is Join?

Join is a kanban-based project management tool developed during a web development bootcamp at the Developer Akademie.

Kanban focuses on visualizing work and tracking progress. Join applies these principles to provide an intuitive interface for planning tasks and monitoring progress.

How to use Join

The Board Overview

The board consists of four columns: To Do, In Progress, Await Feedback, and Done.
